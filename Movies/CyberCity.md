# CyberCity

![poster](../Images/CyberCity.png)

Earth, current year, 2081. The next great danger is the imminent war with 2 powerful entities that plan to subdue the whole plane for an unknown plan. A team led by Maya and Jason is trying to find a plan to stop them, but the odds are against humanity
